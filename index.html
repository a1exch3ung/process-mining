AnalystX Population Health Data and Analytics Centre of Excellence

The Population Health Data and Analytics Centre of Excellence (PHDA CoE) is a place where health and care professionals can find and share knowledge and resources as part of an active community, showcasing real-world examples and applications of the latest developments in AnalystX SQL in collaboration with NHS England's AnalystX programme.

The pandemic has brought together analysis across ICS partners like never before, demonstrating the value of linked data as well as collaborative analytical projects.

To take advantage of this opportunity, ICSs will be bringing together their analytical teams in new ICS intelligence functions, whose aim is to ensure that decisions within an ICS are driven by data.

New skills will be needed to ensure that the health and care system in England is world-leading in the way that analytics inform clinical, operational and strategic decisions. These include skills to build population-based models of care and ensure that ICSs have an understanding of the challenges facing their population over the long term.

To achieve this, the data, analysis and intelligence service in NHSE/I is proposing setting up a Population Health Data and Analytics Centre of Excellence, with the national PHM team. This will support ICS analysts to build the skills needed to design and implement population health analytics to transform services.

The Centre of Excellence will provide training that is available to a wide range of local ICS analysts, utilising a number of partnerships with suppliers. It will build from the AnalystX community, which already has 15,000 members, many of whom are ICS analysts.
